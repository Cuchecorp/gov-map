<objective>
Freeze + shield the DIPID→id_maestra mapping for the ~155 sitting diputados with a fail-closed CI gate, BEFORE the at-scale vote backfill (Phase 66). Purpose: A misattributed vote is defamatory and verifiable as false (risk #1). This phase is the guardrail: it proves the DIPID cross is deterministic-only, fail-closed, and that no name-match/LLM can leak into the vote path.
