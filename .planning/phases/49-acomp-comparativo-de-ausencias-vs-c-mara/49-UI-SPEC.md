## Placement Decision (the load-bearing decision) — LOCKED

**The comparative lives INSIDE the collapsed Votaciones DETAIL (capa-2), as a
dedicated sub-block placed IMMEDIATELY AFTER "Cómo votó" (the sub-block that already
owns the asistencia/ausencia figure), before "Por tema". It does NOT go in capa-1.**

Concretely: a new sub-block rendered inside `VotosView`'s detail `div.space-y-10`,
sequenced `"Cuándo votó"` (F47 chart) → `"Cómo votó"` (breakdown + existing asistencia
line "Presente en X de Y · Ausente en Z") → **`ACOMP comparative`** → `"Por tema"` →
arcos → `"Votó distinto a su bancada"`. Capa-1 (`VotosCapa1`: 5 Mono figures +
"Cómo votó" stacked bar) is unchanged BYTE-FOR-BYTE.

Rationale (five independent reasons, all LOCKED constraints):

1. **F47 precedent is the governing criterion.** F47 placed its visual at the TOP of
   the detail and kept capa-1 byte-identical. The F47 rule reads: "si es UNA línea
   corta de texto factual, puede ir en capa-1; si lleva visual, va al detalle." The
   comparative is **NOT one line** — it is a THREE-part factual block (own rate +
   cámara median + coverage caveat). A multi-line factual block with a caveat does not
   meet the "una línea corta" bar for capa-1 → it goes to the detail.
2. **Semantic adjacency to the ausencia figure.** The cámara median is CONTEXT ON the
   ausencia number. "Cómo votó" already carries that number ("Presente en X de Y
   votaciones · Ausente en Z"). Placing the comparative right after it keeps the fact
   and its context together — the median reads as the honest continuation of an
   already-present figure, not a new standalone scorecard.
3. **First-viewport LOCKED (F55).** The always-visible preattentive band is F55-frozen.
   Injecting a THREE-line, async-fetched, conditionally-present comparative into that
   scan band degrades scannability (CONTEXT: "capa-1 no pierde escaneabilidad") and
   pushes the Lobby carril below the fold. Placing it in the collapsed detail keeps the
   first viewport byte-identical to F55/F47.
4. **Degrade containment (deploy-before-apply).** The comparative can VANISH pre-apply
   (`tasa_ausencia_comparada` → PGRST202). A block that appears/disappears across
   deploys in the always-visible band makes the preattentive layer unstable. Inside the
   default-collapsed detail, the degrade is invisible until the user opens the detail;
   capa-1 stays byte-identical regardless of apply state (CONTEXT: "la capa-1 actual
   queda intacta").
5. **Percentage-as-verdict hazard is lowest in the read-layer.** A standalone "0,7% vs
   mediana 3,2%" pair in the big-Mono glance band reads like a scorecard AT A GLANCE —
   exactly the anti-insinuación risk the SIGNOFF forbids. In the read-not-glanced detail
   layer, next to the neutral "Cómo votó" breakdown, the percentages read as facts among
   facts. This is why the % is ALSO never colored (see Color).

Discarded alternative: "comparativo de UNA línea en capa-1 bajo las cifras". Rejected:
the copy contract is irreducibly multi-line (rate + median + caveat), and reasons 3–5
forbid a conditionally-present scorecard in the frozen preattentive band.

## The Comparative — Contract

### Component
- A new PURE presentation sub-block inside the Votaciones detail (RTL-testable with
  fixtures, no Supabase runtime), mirroring the existing "Cómo votó" asistencia line
  and the `ResumenLinea` pattern in `votos-por-parlamentario.tsx`.
- Receives ONLY a flat serialized shape (numbers/strings) computed server-side from the
  new RPC — never imports the server-only Supabase client. If the shape is `null`
  (RPC absent / degrade), the sub-block renders NOTHING (honest omission).
- NO chart, NO visual encoding, NO bar. Text + Mono figures only.

### Shape (final columns = Claude's discretion; MUST carry the honest universe)
The RPC `tasa_ausencia_comparada(p_parlamentario_id text)` emits, at minimum:
own ausencias `N`, own votaciones total `M`, own rate `X%` (derivable from N/M),
cámara median `Y%`, and the median cohort size `K` (parlamentarios of the SAME cámara
with ≥1 vote in the ingested universe). Never a fabricated number: if any component is
missing/null, the surface omits that line rather than inventing it.

### Number formatting (es-CL, 1 decimal — parity with capa-1 asistencia)
- Percentages use the existing capa-1 formatter convention: `Intl.NumberFormat("es-CL",
  { minimumFractionDigits: 1, maximumFractionDigits: 1 })` → comma decimal, e.g.
  `0,7%`, `3,2%`. Counts (`N`, `M`, `K`) are integers.
- ALL of `N`, `M`, `X%`, `Y%`, `K` render in **Geist Mono** (`font-mono tabular-nums`),
  matching every other figure in this section. No `font-bold`/700 anywhere.

### Anti-Insinuación Invariants (LOCKED — carry forward from F55/F47 + SIGNOFF)
Negative-match enforced (banned-vocab test must stay green). This phase EXTENDS the
banned set beyond the inherited causal/ranking vocabulary:

- **Extended negative-match (NEW, LOCKED):** `top`, `más ausente`, `peor`,
  `mejor asistencia`, `récord` — ALL PROHIBITED. No ranking, no nominal ranking
  ("top ausentes"), no superlative, no comparative-across-parlamentarios framing.
- Inherited (F55/F47/SIGNOFF): ZERO causal vocabulary (influencia, conexión, presión,
  a cambio de, gracias a, porque, afinidad, gestionó, score, ranking,
  tendencia-afirmada). The median is a neutral distributional fact, never a verdict.
- **The percentage is NEVER a verdict.** No adjective qualifies the rate. The universe
  is stated explicitly (N/M own + K cohort) and the coverage caveat is mandatory 1×.
- **The % is NOT colored** (see Color) — a high % rendered red = implicit verdict,
  PROHIBITED.
- Counts and the median are neutral facts SIDE BY SIDE; the comparative never composes
  ausencia with a vote sense, a meeting, or any other domain.

| Degrade (LOCKED, mirror `cruces-de-proyecto.tsx`) | `error?.code === "PGRST202"` (function-not-found, pre-apply) → **render NOTHING** (comparative omitted, capa-1 + rest of detail intact). Deploy MAY precede apply. |

| Render | Server fetches + passes the flat shape (or null) as a prop; the sub-block is a pure presentation component. SSR intact; no client island needed (no chart). |
| Disclosure | Inside the existing `DetalleColapsable` (default CLOSED). No new toggle, no lazy-fetch, no round-trip. |
